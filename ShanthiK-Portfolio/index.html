Puddles gathering below the wilted maple trees

Quenching the thirst of the withered and shriveled leaves

Awakening them from their deep slumber.

Smooth pebbles glisten as the yellow street light glares upon its wet exterior

Window screens drip a drop,

Spotlessly Cleaning the cemented patios .

Leaving me to endlessly stare at this play of nature

Filling the insides of me with a deep sense of calm.

Wishing this rainy night would never end.

Whats not to love about this season

Filled with all the right reason.

A majestic show of spectacular colors galore

Fiery red, rusty browns, bright yellows

Competing with one another to stand out.

The synchronized spectrum of colors,

Defining the perfect nature in harmony.

Leaves hanging onto the tree just one more time, just one more bit

Before being roughly knocked down by the cool, breezy fierce wind

Nothing can stop them, nothing in vain

Raining leaves forming a golden carpet of intermixed colors.

People naturally drawn to walk on the transformed magical path.

Reminding one to ease down in their fast paced tracks,

Reminding one to instill themselves with new hope,

Life that begins anew.

I slow down, I stand still

Beneath such a tree in all its colorful glory

I allow the wind to settle the leaves gently down on me

Some rest upon on my head, some on my shoulders, some sway down to my feet.

I continue to stand rock still, not wanting to move a muscle.

I soak it all in , I savor what the mystical moment had to offer me.
